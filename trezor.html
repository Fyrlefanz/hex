Welcome to the Trezor FreeClaiming Guide

To claim using a Trezor you will need Electrum. Electrum is needed to sign messages. Remember: you will not be exporting/exposing your private keys. They will stay on your Trezor and there is no chance to get compromised. You can download Electrum here: https://electrum.org/#download

1. Once downloaded, start Electrum. You will be greeted with the following screen:

2. On the next screen, choose Standard Wallet and press next.

3. Make sure your Trezor is connected to your PC. Your Trezor needs to be unlocked and you need to have the Bitcoin App open on your Trezor Device. Then choose the bottom option: Use a hardware device and press next.

4. After a few seconds Electrum should detect your Trezor. Press next.

5. Now we have to choose the type of addresses in your wallet. If your addresses start with a "1" -> choose legacy (p2pkh) If your addresses start with a "3" -> choose p2sh-segit (p2wpkh-p2sh) If your addresses start with "bc1" -> choose native segwit (p2wpkh)

Don't change anything in the derivation path. Click next.

On the file encryption window, just press next.

After a few seconds you should get to the main Electrum window. Go to the top and click on "View" -> click on "Show Addresses".

This should give you a new tab next to "Receive" called "Addresses". Click on that. Now you should see all of the Bitcoin Addresses your Trezor uses. You can see the balances of each address and the number of transactions (Tx) on the right side

We have to claim each address with a balance 1-by-1. Choose the top address with a balance and rightclick it (doublefingerclick on MacOS) and choose "Copy Address"

Now go to the HEX Website using this link (You will get 10% more HEX using our link): https://go.hex.win/?r=0xUnsereAddresse

Make sure to log into MetaMask and allow MetaMask to connect to the Website.

Then click on the "Claim" tab in the top. Paste your address into the field and press on the "Claim This Address" button

You will see a message that looks like this: Claim_HEX_to_YourETHAddress Copy this message by clicking on the circled icon.

Then go back to Electrum. Rightclick on your address and choose "Sign/verify message"

Paste your copied message into the top field called "Message" Make sure your Trezor is still connected, logged in and that you have the Bitcoin App open on the device. Then press on the "Sign" button in the bottom left.

After a few seconds a Message hash should appear. Check if the Message hash matches the one shown on your Trezor Device. If it matches, confirm the signing proccess on your Trezor.

Now you should see a signature in the "Signature" field. Copy the complete signature.

Go back to the website and paste your signature into the "Signature" field.

Now you can choose the number of days you want to stake. For FreeClaims the minimum number of days is 350. Maximum stake length is 5555 days. To get maximum bonuses you have to choose a stakelength of 3641 days or more. Once you have chosen your stakelength click on the "Submit Claim" button. Confirm the transaction in MetaMask and wait for it to confirm. Congratulations, you have successfully FreeClaimed HEX.
